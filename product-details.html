    Chicken ala kiev

    Chicken a la Kiev is a dish where a chicken breast is pounded thin, stuffed with flavored butter, breaded, and then fried or baked. When the dish is cut, the butter melts out, creating a rich and flavorful experience. The stuffed breast is then breaded and fried or baked until golden brown and cooked through. The dish is known for its rich, buttery flavor, often with a hint of garlic and herbs from the butter filling.

    Key Components:
    • Chicken Breast – Boneless and skinless, pounded thin.
    • Garlic Herb Butter (Compound Butter) – Made with butter, garlic, parsley, salt, and sometimes lemon juice or dill.
    • Coating – Typically involves a flour dredge, egg wash, and breadcrumbs.
    • Cooking Method – Traditionally deep-fried, though it can be baked as a lighter option.
    Key Components:
    • Crispy exterior
    • Juicy chicken
    • Melted, aromatic butter center
